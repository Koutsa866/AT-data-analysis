1. Historical Injury Records

  • Past 2-3 years of injury data (any format you have)
  • Include serious injuries (surgeries, fractures, long recoveries)
  • Actual return dates (to improve recovery time predictions, if they were recorded)

2. Basic Athlete Information

  • Sport and position (from team rosters)
  • Age, height, weight (standard athlete data)
  • Previous injury history (you already track this)
Why: Different sports/positions have different injury and recovery patterns

3. Training Context

  • Practice vs. game injuries (simple classification)
  • Season timing (pre-season, in-season, off-season)
  • Playing time (exposure differences)
Why: Helps predict when injuries are most likely
